Welcome

Meet Joseph P. Cadicina

Joseph P. Cadicina is a member of Laufer Dalena Cadicina Jensen & Boyd LLC and concentrates his practice in the area of matrimonial/family law. He previously served as a judicial clerk to the Honorable Mark M. Russello, J.S.C., Family Division, Bergen County. Mr. Cadicina is admitted to practice law in New Jersey, New York and Washington, D.C. He received his undergraduate degree from Seton Hall University and attended Thomas M. Cooley Law School where he obtained a Juris Doctorate Degree with Distinction.Mr. Cadicina is a member of the Bergen and Morris County Bar Associations, Family Law Sections, as well as the New Jersey State Bar Associations. In addition, he is a member of the Northern New Jersey Family Law Inns of Court. Mr. Cadicina is a Court Approved Economic Mediator for Family Law Cases and performs private divorce mediation for clients. He is also a volunteer panelist for the Morris County Early Settlement Program. Mr. Cadicina serves on the Board of Directors for the Northern New Jersey American Heart Association and its Gala Committee.
